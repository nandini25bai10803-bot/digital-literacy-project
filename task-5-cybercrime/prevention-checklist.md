# &#x20;Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

&#x20;General Safety Tips



Always verify the sender’s email address or phone number before clicking on any links or downloading attachments.



Check website URLs carefully (look for https:// and correct spelling) before entering login details.



Never share sensitive information such as passwords, OTPs, or bank details with anyone.



Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts like email, social media, and banking apps.



Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts and update them regularly.



Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into personal or financial accounts.



Be cautious of messages that create urgency (e.g., “account will be blocked”) as they are often scams.



Install apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or App Store.



&#x20;UPI / Financial Safety Tips



Always double-check the UPI ID and recipient name before sending money.



Never approve unknown or unexpected payment requests on apps like Google Pay or PhonePe.



Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown individuals or from untrusted sources.



Remember: receiving money does NOT require entering a PIN — only sending money does.



&#x20;Reporting Cybercrime



Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in

Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24×7)

Immediately inform your bank if you suspect any financial fraud
